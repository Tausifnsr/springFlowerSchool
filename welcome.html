Principal's Message:

Dear Parents, Teachers and Students, Change is a desirous thing and all of us want change in life in one or the other way. Education is one field where educationists, parents and students constantly look for change with respect to evolving contexts and challenges. Michael Fullan, a noted educationist talks of three areas where change could occur: the possible use of new materials (curriculum materials and technologies), the possible use of new teaching approaches and the possible alteration of beliefs. Spring Flowers with a change in leadership looks forward to carry ahead these notions and the Ignatian legacy... - Gilbert Johnson
